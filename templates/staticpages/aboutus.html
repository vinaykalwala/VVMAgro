About VVM Agro Industries

Pioneering Agricultural Machinery and Tractors Since 2016

Our Journey

Founded on 27-Jun-2016, VVM Agro Industries is a Micro enterprise (2023-24) dedicated to transforming agriculture and electrical contracting through innovative equipment. Our journey began in Hyderabad, Telangana, and expanded to Hayathanagar, Ranga Reddy District, Telangana, with additional operations in Mancherial, Telangana.

Under our V VMotors division, we manufacture high-performance tractors ranging from 33 HP to 90 HP and serve as an authorized state distributor for Indofarm Tractor spare parts across Telangana & Andhra Pradesh. Our innovative Hole Lifter, designed for electrical contractors, digs holes up to 6 feet and lifts poles (8m, 9.1m, 11m), significantly reducing manpower and costs.

We also specialize in:

  • Wood working machines for precision processing.
  • Tractor attachments for efficient agricultural tasks.
  • Waste recycling machines for sustainable horticulture.
  • Forestry tools for advanced operations.

With tie-ups with banks and finance companies, we ensure accessible solutions for our customers, backed by excellent after-sales service.

Our Vision

At VVM Agro Industries, our vision is to lead the global agricultural and electrical equipment industry by offering high multi-featured, fuel-efficient solutions at optimum cost. We aim to deliver world-class performance, backed by exceptional after-sales services, empowering farmers and contractors with innovative tools that enhance productivity and sustainability.

We strive to:

  • Pioneer sustainable technologies that reduce environmental impact.
  • Expand our reach to support agricultural communities worldwide.
  • Foster innovation through continuous research and development.
  • Build lasting partnerships with customers, prioritizing their success.

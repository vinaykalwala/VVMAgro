Pesticide Sprayer

Product Overview

Our state-of-the-art pesticide sprayer is designed for efficient and uniform application of pesticides, herbicides, and other agricultural chemicals. With advanced features and robust construction, it ensures optimal coverage while minimizing waste and environmental impact.

  • High capacity tank with chemical-resistant coating
  • Adjustable spray nozzles for precise application
  • User-friendly controls and comfortable handling
  • Durable construction for long-lasting performance
  • Reduced drift technology for environmental safety

  • Power Requirement: 30-50 HP
  • Operating Pressure: 2-5 bar
